Why HTTPS Matters

HTTPS encrypts data between the browser and server. It prevents hackers from reading or stealing private information.

Benefits of HTTPS

  • Protects passwords and personal data.
  • Prevents tampering with web pages.
  • Builds trust for online shopping and banking.
